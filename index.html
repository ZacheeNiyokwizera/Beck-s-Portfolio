Rebecca Tara Cameron

Agile Facilitator | Legal Advisor

I was born in England, moved to Malawi and now call South Africa my home.
I studied law with a sprinkling of Psychology, traveled the world and eventually got a real job.
I started a group called “F*ck the Cape town Clique” to promote prosocial behaviour, build a community and quite frankly, make friends.
You could say my love language is gift giving, but mostly gift receiving.
I am a qualified attorney and finished my articles of Clerkship at CDH.
I then did what a millennial does and changed careers inspired by discussions at Lean (Startup) Coffee.
After that, I got to work for one of South Africa’s most awesome start-ups called OfferZen and learned about the Tech World.
It was there that I discovered this term called “Agile”: a career where my job is to talk to people all day and get paid for it #mindblown.
I now work at Afrolabs as an Agile Facilitator and Legal Advisor combining the worlds of law and tech.
I have since started a meetup called Lean (legal) coffee hosted at CDH (and now remotely).
I am also on the committee for SUGSA : we organise so many activities for Agilists.
I would love to visit your office because I love meetings and free coffee.
